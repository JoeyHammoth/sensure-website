Skill Transfer of Tactile-Based Experience
We are introducing a groundbreaking solution to bridge the gap between expert and novice clinicians in tactile-based procedures. By translating mechanical vibrations into auditory feedback, our platform enables trainees to perceive and practice tactile cues that are traditionally gained only through years of hands-on experience. This innovative approach accelerates skill acquisition, ensuring clinicians develop the confidence and accuracy needed for complex procedures. Supervisors gain the ability to monitor and guide in real-time, enhancing the efficacy of training and mentorship.
